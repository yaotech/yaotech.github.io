About Us

YaoTech is comprised of a team of software engineers and dreamers whoses common goal is transforming the technological capability of Kenya and Sub-Saharan Africa.

Our goal is to create software solutions to augment the existing solutions and most importantly creating software that promotes fast and equal access to information, resources and services.

We pride ourselves in creating software that gives users unique and new perspective
